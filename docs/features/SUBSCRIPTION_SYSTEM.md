# Subscription System

Our application offers a subscription-based model to provide different levels of access to our features. This document outlines the available plans and the features associated with each.

## Plans

We offer the following subscription plans:

- **Free**: Perfect for getting started. Includes basic music generation capabilities and a limited number of projects.
- **Pro**: For serious creators. Unlocks advanced AI features, reference audio uploads, and more projects.
- **Studio**: For professional studios. Provides access to all features, including our most advanced AI tools and a higher project limit.
- **Enterprise**: For businesses and large teams. Offers custom solutions, dedicated support, and API access.

## Feature Gating

Access to certain features is restricted based on your subscription plan. If you attempt to use a feature that is not available on your current plan, you will be prompted to upgrade.

## Managing Your Subscription

You can manage your subscription at any time from the "Subscription" page in your workspace. Here, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan, view your current usage, and see your billing history.
